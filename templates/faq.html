About URL Safety Checker

URL Safety Checker is a free online tool that allows you to check if a website URL is safe before visiting it. Our service analyzes URLs for potential security threats such as phishing attempts, malware, and other harmful content.

This project was created by Mehebubur Rahman and Rudradev Choudhury, Class 12 students of PM SHRI KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA BARPETA, as part of a school project under the guidance of their teacher.

URL Safety Checker is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers.

Using the Service

Simply enter the URL you want to check in the input field on our homepage and click the "Scan URL" button. Our system will analyze the URL and provide you with a detailed safety report.

Our URL checker uses multiple detection methods to provide accurate results. However, it's important to note that no security tool is 100% perfect. We recommend using our service as one layer in your overall online safety approach.

Currently, our service only supports checking one URL at a time. This allows us to provide more detailed analysis for each individual URL.

Privacy & Security

We do not permanently store the URLs you submit for checking. URLs are processed in memory and are automatically deleted after analysis is complete. For more information, please see our Privacy Policy.
